🔋 What is an On-Grid Solar System?

An on-grid solar system is connected to the utility grid. It generates solar electricity during the day, powering your appliances directly and feeding excess energy to the grid. You get credited for this power, effectively lowering your electricity bill.

It’s the most cost-effective and popular solar solution for urban areas with stable grid availability.
